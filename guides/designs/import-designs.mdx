title: Importing Designs
description: "How to import Designs from store.print.app"
---

In this article, you will receive step-by-step instructions on how to import designs from the Print.App store to your own store.

1.  To get started, log into your Print.App account and navigate to the [Designs section](https://admin.print.app/designs)

2.  Click the Import Designs button, this should load the Store in a side bar as shown below:
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/design-import.png" />
    </Frame>

3.  Browse through the design collection and click the Plus button against any design you wish to import. You can also preview the designs or download the PDF output directly from the store.

4.  Click the big Green button at the bottom right hand corner labeled *"Add selected Designs to your store"*. This should clone the Designs into your store and you can assign them to your products or edit as desired.
